About Us

Bolide Infrastructure Solutions Limited (BIS) is a Zambian-owned construction company established in 2019. We deliver integrated construction, steel fabrication, and civil infrastructure solutions for industrial, commercial, public-sector, and residential clients.

Our strength lies in combining local market knowledge with international standards. We source materials from South Africa and China, ensuring compliance with SABS and globally recognized specifications.

Core Services

Structural Steel Fabrication & Erection
Design coordination, fabrication, supply, and erection of structural steel for warehouses, industrial facilities, commercial buildings, and bespoke steel structures. All works executed in compliance with SABS and relevant engineering standards.
Civil Engineering & Road Works
Construction and rehabilitation of road infrastructure including earthworks, grading, compaction, drainage systems, culverts, and crossing points for urban and rural applications.
Fencing & Perimeter Security Systems
Supply and installation of clear view fencing, chain link fencing, diamond wire, access-controlled gates, and high-security perimeter solutions for industrial, institutional, and commercial sites.
Industrial Grating & Handrail Systems
Fabrication and installation of galvanized and mild steel grating, staircases, platforms, walkways, and handrails designed to meet industrial safety and access requirements.
Container Offices & Modular Structures
Design, supply, and installation of prefabricated container offices, accommodation units, clinics, and site facilities including electrical, plumbing, insulation, and interior finishes.
Building Construction & Fit-Out Works
End-to-end residential and commercial building works including structural construction, interior finishes, aluminium doors and windows, and supporting building services.
